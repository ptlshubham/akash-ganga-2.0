Meet Our Founders

Welcome to our leading poultry farm! We supply high-quality eggs and poultry to local farmers in the region. Our commitment to superior products and service has made us a trusted partner in the agricultural industry. We specialize in providing near-hatch eggs to poultry farmers, along with a range of services such as breed selection, barn design, and feeding advice. We also offer all the necessary equipment for starting and operating a farm.

Our farm follows sustainable and ethical practices, providing a healthy and natural environment for our birds. With experienced farmers and animal welfare professionals on our team, we provide personalized service to meet each client's unique needs. We operate both manual and automated farms, accommodating up to 4 lakh chickens. Additionally, we excel in breeding and caring for broilers, focusing on ethical farming practices and disease prevention.
