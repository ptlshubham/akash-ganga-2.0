Welcome To Suguna Foods

Suguna Foods Pvt. Ltd, India’s leading poultry conglomerate, has grown to become a INR 10,750 crore turnover company, over a period of 35+ years. The company’s core value is its dedication to economic and social development in rural India, and it has currently empowered over 40,000+ farmers from 15,000+ villages across 18 Indian states, rewriting the destiny of thousands of farmers and creating our future entrepreneurs. It mobilizes to be the 'protein leader' and bridges with adept business ideals that strives to build healthier nation to consumers
