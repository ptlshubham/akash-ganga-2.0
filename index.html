Welcome to Akashganga Poultry Farm!

We are a leading poultry farm committed to providing high-quality products and services. Our chickens are raised in a natural and healthy environment and fed a nutritious and balanced diet, resulting in the highest-quality products including fresh eggs, chicken meat, and processed chicken products.

In addition to our products, we offer expert advice and guidance on poultry farming, and training and support to new farmers. We are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of animal welfare and environmental sustainability. Thank you for choosing Akashganga Poultry Farm for your poultry needs.

Our Services
